About

Computer Science & Engineering

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering is renowned for original research and for imparting state-of-the-art world-class education. The department of Computer Science & Engineering imparts quality training and research. Students who have a quest for programming, software and web development, software, and web designing, data analytics can actively be a part of this course. This Department is designed in such a manner that the computer science program orients students towards the pragmatic approach of computer science and provides the learning and practices to make them proficient computing professionals.
