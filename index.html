lAB

A computer science lab is a dedicated space equipped with computers, software, and hardware resources for practical experimentation, programming, and research in the field of computer science. It provides students and researchers with hands-on experience, fostering learning and innovation in areas such as programming, software development, and computer system.

SEMINAR HALL

A seminar hall is a designated space within an institution or facility designed to host seminars, conferences, lectures, and presentations. It is equipped with audio-visual aids, seating arrangements, and technical support to facilitate effective communication and interaction during educational and professional events.

CLASS ROOM

A college classroom is a designated space within an educational institution where academic instruction and learning activities take place. Typically equipped with desks or tables and seating arrangements for students, it also features a teaching area for the instructor. Classrooms are equipped with whiteboards, blackboards, or other visual aids, as well as audio-visual equipment to support teaching and presentations.
